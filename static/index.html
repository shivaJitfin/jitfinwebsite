About Us

Supply Chain Finance is a crucial aspect of the larger picture of supply chain management. By connecting suppliers and distributors with access to timely funds, our tailored finance solutions can help corporate anchors lower financing costs and increase overall operational efficiency. Critically, Supply Chain Finance unlocks working capital that is tied up in the supply chain. At JitFinCo, we recognize the importance of MSMEs to the economy and strive towards providing inclusive financing solutions that can ensure mutual growth and development.
